Advocate

We work with Congress, Federal agencies, and
partners on policy issues and legislation to ensure
funding for trails, preservation of natural areas, and
protection of the hiking experience.

Volunteer

Trails don't maintain themselves. Individuals of
any age and experience level can become trail
stewards. Give back to the trails you love, meet
new people, and enjoy the experience!

Explore

Feel confident when you set out on your next
trail adventure. Check out our free hiking
resources like tips for hiking with families, gear
checklists, finding the best trail for you.
